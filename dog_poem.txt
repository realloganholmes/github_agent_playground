Meow, meow, the cats do say,
Whiskered friends who softly play.
Pouncing high and creeping around,
Silly kitties, they meow and bound.

On the windowsill, they stretch so free,
Watching birds, as cute as can be.
Tabbies, calicos, and Siamese too,
Every cat is special, it’s true.

With gentle paws and playful purrs,
They fill our lives with loving whirs.
Companions gentle, both small and grand,
In the hearts of cat lovers, they always stand.

So here’s to the cats, our furry friends,
With love and joy that never ends.
