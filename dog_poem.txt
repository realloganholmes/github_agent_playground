Oh the dog, so loyal and true,
A friend that's always there for you.
With wagging tail and joyful bark,
In every heart, they leave their mark.

Through fields they run, with eyes so bright,
Chasing dreams from morning till night.
They cuddle close, in moments of peace,
In their warm love, all worries cease.

With playful paws and a gentle nudge,
In times of joy, they never judge.
Their love is pure, a gift so grand,
With them, you’ll always have a hand.

So here’s to dogs, both big and small,
In our lives, they bring the best of all.
A wagging tail, a heart so rare,
Forever our friends, beyond compare!
