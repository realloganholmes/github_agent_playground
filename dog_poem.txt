Woof, woof, the dogs do say,
Furry friends who love to play.
Chasing tails and bounding around,
Silly pups, they jump and bound.

In the park, they run so free,
Jumping high, they bark with glee.
Golden retrievers, poodles too,
Every dog is special, it's true.

With wagging tails and playful barks,
They light up our lives, like bright, shining sparks.
Companions loyal, through thick and thin,
In the hearts of dog lovers, they always win.

So here's to the dogs, our furry friends,
With love and joy that never ends.
