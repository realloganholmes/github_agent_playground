In a world where paws tread lightly,
Felines frolic, oh so sprightly.
With tails held high and eyes aglow,
They dance through shadows, putting on a show.

Whiskers twitch with playful grace,
In sunlit corners, they find their place.
While dogs may bark and play with glee,
Cats lounge in calm tranquility.

On soft cushions, they curl and purr,
In cozy nooks, they quietly stir.
Yet when the moon casts silver beams,
They leap to life, fulfilling dreams.

A gentle nudge, a curious tap,
A feline's charm, a perfect trap.
In a house of barks, they rule the roost,
With silent steps, they're aptly seduced.

So let’s raise a toast to our whiskered friends,
In a world of dogs, where the fun never ends.
For in their hearts, they share a tale,
Of friendship forged, in every tail.
