I love my dog, he's my best friend,
With wagging tail, our joys transcend.
Through fields we run, both wild and free,
Together in our harmony.

His joyful bark, a melody sweet,
With every leap, he dances on his feet.
In sunlit days and starry nights,
My furry companion brings me delights.

When shadows fall and sunsets glow,
He's by my side, our bond will grow.
With every lick and playful tease,
A love so deep, it puts me at ease.

Through every storm and every trial,
He lifts my spirit, makes me smile.
In packs or solo, he's never afar,
My loyal friend, my guiding star.
