Oh, dear dogs, with tails that wag,
You bring such joy, you never lag.
With playful barks and loving eyes,
In your presence, my heart flies.

You greet me with a happy dance,
In every leap, you take a chance.
Through muddy parks or sunny trails,
With you, life never fails.

Your loyalty, a shining light,
In darkest times, you are my knight.
A furry friend, so pure and true,
Oh, how I love, I cherish you.

From poodles small to great danes tall,
Each one of you, I love them all.
So here’s a rhyme, a heartfelt cheer,
To all the dogs I hold so dear!
