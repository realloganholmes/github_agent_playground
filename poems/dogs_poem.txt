Barking happily, tails a-wag,
Loyal friends, never a drag.
Running through fields, playful and free,
Dogs bring joy, just wait and see!

Chasing after squirrels, jumping high,
Under the vast and open sky.
With every woof, and every bark,
They light up our lives, igniting a spark.

Furry companions, in sunshine or rain,
Through thick and thin, they ease our pain.
With loving eyes and hearts so true,
A dog's love is a treasure, always new.
