Purring softly, sleek and sly,
Majestic creatures, oh my, oh my!
Chasing shadows, leaping high,
Cats bring wonders, as they sigh.

Curled in sunbeams, dreams so sweet,
Whiskers twitching, light on their feet.
With every purr, a soothing sound,
Building a bond that’s truly profound.

Feline friends, in quiet grace,
Home is a joy, with each soft face.
Through every pounce and gentle nudge,
A cat’s love is a warm, sweet hug.
