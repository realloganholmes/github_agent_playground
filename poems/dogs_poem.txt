In the sun, they stretch and purr,
Whiskers twitching, they softly stir.
With graceful leaps and quiet grace,
A gentle touch, a warm embrace.

Curled up tight in a sunny spot,
Chasing beams when they’re in the mood.
Furry masters of the nap,
In every heart, they find their map.

With playful paws, they bat and chase,
Little hunters, leaving no trace.
In the night, they prowl and roam,
Every shadow feels like home.

So here’s to the cats, both sleek and sly,
With their soft mews and watchful eye.
In our lives, they bring such joy,
Feline friends, we all enjoy!
