In the yard, they run and play,
Furry friends, brightening my day.
With wagging tails and playful barks,
They fill our lives with joyful sparks.

Golden fur and eager eyes,
Chasing shadows under the skies.
Always there, loyal and true,
A bond so special, just me and you.

Through muddy puddles, we leap and bound,
In every corner, happiness found.
Through seasons change, they stay by my side,
In their warm love, I gently abide.

So here’s to the dogs, both big and small,
With their love and joy, they enrich us all.
In our hearts, they will forever remain,
Best friends for life, through joy and pain.
