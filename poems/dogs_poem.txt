When a dog wags its tail with glee,
It's a signal that they’re happy to see.
With floppy ears and a barking sound,
A best friend is what you have found.

In the park, they run and play,
Chasing balls and enjoying the day.
With loyalty that knows no bounds,
In a dog's heart, true love abounds.

Through rain or shine, they stand by you,
A furry friend, so loyal and true.
So here's to dogs, our playful pals,
The joy they bring, in barks and howls!
