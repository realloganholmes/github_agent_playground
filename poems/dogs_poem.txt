Cats are graceful, sleek and bright,
With eyes that shimmer in the night.
They pounce and play, so full of glee,
Curled up in sunbeams, wild and free.

Their tiny paws and gentle purrs,
Whiskers twitching, as beauty stirs.
From chasing yarn to climbing high,
Cats bring joy, oh my, oh my!

Soft fur coats that warm our hearts,
In silent ways, they play their parts.
With every leap and every bound,
In their sweet company, love is found.
