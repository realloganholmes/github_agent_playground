The dog, a faithful friend so true,
With wagging tail and playful view.
In sunny fields, they run and play,
Chasing balls and light of day.

With gentle eyes and hearts so warm,
They bring us cheer, they are our charm.
Through thick and thin, they stand by us,
A bond so deep, a special trust.

In every bark, a story flows,
Of loyalty, as everyone knows.
So here’s to dogs, our furry mates,
In every heart, their love creates.
