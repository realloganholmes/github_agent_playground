In the quiet corners, they softly tread,
Whiskers twitching, in dreams they’re fed.
With a purr that’s soothing, they light up the night,
Furry companions, a pure delight.

Their playful antics, a dance of grace,
Chasing shadows, they quicken the pace.
With every leap and every bound,
In their tender presence, pure joy is found.

So here’s to the cats, so clever and bright,
With love in our hearts, they’re a true delight.
