The cat sat on the mat,
Purring softly, chasing a rat.
With eyes so bright and fur so sleek,
A graceful hunter, quiet, unique.

Whiskers twitching, tail held high,
The master of stealth as she roams nigh.
In sunlit patches, she'll curl up tight,
In dreams of adventures, she'll pounce in the night.

Oh, lovely feline, so regal and proud,
You enchant us all, drawing a crowd.
With each gentle paw and soft little meow,
You bring us comfort, oh yes you know how.
