The cat stretches wide, in sunlight so warm,
With purrs and soft nudges, it’s always a charm.
A creature of grace, with a mysterious gaze,
In their playful antics, we find endless ways.

With whispers of paws on the soft, quiet floor,
They curl up beside us, we couldn't ask for more.
In shadows they leap, in the moon's gentle light,
Cats steal our hearts, in the day and the night.
