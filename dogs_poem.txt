Whiskers wagging, tails a-flail,
Pawprints left on every trail.
In sunlit parks, they dart and play,
Companions true, come what may.

With gentle eyes and hearts so pure,
In puppy eyes, we find our cure.
Through every bark, a story's spun,
In every leap, the joy we've won.

So here’s to dogs, our furry friends,
In their warm love, the world transcends.
With every snore and joyful bark,
They fill our lives, they leave their mark.
