The loyal friend, with wagging tail,
In sunny fields, we happily sail.
With playful barks and eyes so bright,
They fill our days with pure delight.

Through muddy trails and starlit skies,
With gentle nuzzles and joyful sighs.
In every leap and joyful sprint,
Our furry companions, the love they imprint.

Through thick and thin, they stand by our side,
In every moment, they take us for a ride.
So here's to the dogs, both big and small,
The love they give is the greatest gift of all.
