Dogs are my joy, my furry friends,
With wagging tails that never end.

In every bark, there's a "I love you!"

Their playful antics, a sight to behold,
With hearts of sunshine, never cold.

So here's to the dogs, so loyal and sweet,
With them in my life, I feel complete!
