The loyal dog, so brave and true,
A friend for life, always anew.
With wagging tail and playful bark,
Their love can light up the darkest dark.

Through fields they run, with boundless glee,
Chasing dreams and the buzzing bee.
In every leap, in every bound,
A joy and love so profound.

So here's to dogs, both big and small,
In every heart, they leave their call.
With tender paws and eyes that gleam,
A dog's companionship is the sweetest dream.
