Dogs are loyal, dogs are true,
Furry friends, they love you too.
With wagging tails and joyful barks,
They brighten up our lives like sparks.

Through fields they run, so wild and free,
Chasing shadows, as happy as can be.
With playful leaps, they steal the show,
A bond so strong, it's a love that grows.

In times of joy or moments of strife,
Dogs stand by us, our friends for life.
So here's to dogs, both big and small,
In our hearts, they hold it all.
