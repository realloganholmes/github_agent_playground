Whiskers twitch, a silent pounce,
In shadows they gracefully bounce.
With a flick of a tail and a soft little meow,
Cats do as they please, they know just how.

Sunbeams dance on their furry backs,
As they nap in a patch, avoiding the wracks.
Curled up tight in a cozy ball,
A kingdom of dreams where they rule over all.

They chase after strings and leap high in the air,
With a playful spirit, nothing can compare.
Purring softly, they melt every heart,
A bond with these creatures, you never will part.

So here’s to the cats, so regal and sly,
With elegance and grace, they effortlessly fly.
Each moment with them is a treasure, it’s true,
In the world of pets, cats reign supreme, it’s you!
