In a world where tails wag loud and proud,
Cats roam free, often lost in a crowd,
On the dogs' branch, they prance with grace,
With a flick of a tail and a furry embrace.

Whiskers twitching, eyes sharp and bright,
They weave through the barks, a curious sight.
Dogs might bark, but cats hold their ground,
In their elegant dance, peace can be found.

Paws padded softly, on grass lush and green,
Cats on the branch, in a world unforeseen.
While dogs chase their tails with unbridled glee,
A cat rests calmly, just wanting to be free.

So here's to the felines, on dog’s merry way,
They bring tales of mischief and dreams in the day.
With a purr as their anthem and a leap as their song,
On the dogs' happy branch, the cats still belong.
